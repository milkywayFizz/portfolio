About Me

I am a second-year Computer Science student at the University of L'Aquila (UnivAQ).

My journey in Information Technology began with a fundamental goal: achieving Digital Sovereignty. What started as a practical effort to optimize personal devices and mitigate corporate telemetry has evolved into a rigorous academic and professional pursuit. I am deeply interested in system architecture, data privacy, and building secure infrastructures from the ground up.

Core Focus & Career Goals

My objective is to specialize in areas where system performance and proactive security intersect:

  • Blue Teaming & Network Defense: Traffic analysis and infrastructure monitoring.
  • Device Optimization & Hardening: Attack surface reduction and bare-metal OS tuning.
  • DevSecOps & Cloud Security: Designing sovereign and automated cloud environments.
